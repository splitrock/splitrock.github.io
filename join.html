OVERVIEW

INSTEAD OF SEARCHING FOR THE PERFECT INTERNSHIP, BUILD ONE

We are looking for creative students and entrepreneurs to join our small startup creative services shop based near the University of Florida campus in Gainesville, FL. We’re looking for business-focused students with an interest in creative work and design.

The internship is project-based: working with the company owners, professional mentors, and a team of other students, the intern will develop a plan for an entrepreneurship-based project, then complete the project following a set timeline during the summer of 2017. The internship is paid on stipend and has a flexible schedule. Training will be provided for any technical skills required. (The skills acquired will be very useful for future internships and jobs.)

HOW IT WORKS

EXPERIENCE

Student teams tackle creative projects for small local clients under the direction and guidance of professional mentors. Projects are:

  • small and reasonably straightforward (not complex) in scope
  • low stakes in terms of scope and critical business functions
  • low stakes in terms of overall budget, but paid (not pro-bono) and self-sufficient

LEARN

Students progress through roles increasing in freedom and accountability.

  • Students are brought in on a per project basis
  • After completing a set of tasks at a high level, students move up to a semester-long role position.
  • Role position students move up to senior-level positions with oversight.
  • Students can also move down (and out) of the group

GROW

The group works on a small number of projects during the year

  • The group cultivates relationships with local agencies and creatives organizations to collect referrals for projects that fit above criteria, often too small to be profitable for the agency.
  • The group cultivates relationships with local entrepreneurs groups
  • Students can propose ideas to the group, which the group will vet the ideas.

Split Rock Studio is your path to new skills and new connections. Here are some of the benefits of our internships:

1. Apply skills you have learned to business situations and get real-world experience with actual clients.
Become a better job candidate because you already know what you'll need to know - you have the experience. Learn how to produce the work the clients are looking for - at the level they are looking for.
2. Build the skills you need for the job / career you want, or explore new skills.
Learn skills in demand in the job market or just try something new. You build a learning program that is customized for *you*.
3. Students can connect with professionals in various fields - build your group of experts you know in your field of interest. Be able to contact mentors to get help on questions.
4. Students will be compensated for working on projects they enjoy doing, while learning skills they’ve always wanted to learn
5. Work with a team of students in a professional setting where everyone is motivated to do their best work.
