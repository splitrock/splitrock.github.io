ABOUT US

ALWAYS LEARNING, ALWAYS EXCEEDING EXPECTATIONS

Split Rock Studio is unique creative learning lab for student creatives and communicators, a place where students and professionals combine to do great work for small business and non-profit clients in our community. We're a hybrid of a traditional agency, modern distributed company and learning apprenticeship. We select the hardest-working students, pair them with great mentors, and set high standards.

WHAT WE DO

WE'RE HERE TO HELP YOU GROW

If you're running a small business, you know there are always tasks left on your to-do list: content production, process documentation, social media advertising. After you've completed all the critical tasks and done the work to get those core projects out the door, it seems there's always a blog post to write or website update to make. And what about that e-book you've been meaning to write?

We understand the time contraints the come with managing a business. We offer quality services at a flat, affordable rate, ensuring that the service we deliver represents YOU and your brand. We'll meet with you and listen carefully to your needs. We'll work hard to understand your story; and when we do, we'll work with you on a plan to help your business. Then we'll create great content, engage your current and future customers, and help you build your business.

OUR SERVICES

EVERYTHING YOU NEED OR WANT

We cater to the services that you need in the time that you require and the price that you can afford.
